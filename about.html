**About Shweta Makeover Studio**

Shweta Makeover Studio, led by Shweta Sharma, is a trusted name in beauty and wellness services in Varanasi. As a skilled beautician and mehndi artist, she offers a wide range of professional services designed to enhance your natural beauty and confidence. With expertise in bridal makeup, party makeup, and creative mehndi designs, the studio specializes in making your special moments truly memorable. In addition to makeup services, we provide complete beauty care including facials, eyebrow shaping, waxing, bleaching, pedicure, manicure, hair cutting, hair styling, hair spa, head massage, and D-tan treatments. We are proud to offer convenient home services, bringing salon-quality care right to your doorstep for maximum comfort and ease. Located at Pahari Gate, BLW, Varanasi, Shweta Makeover Studio is committed to using quality products, maintaining hygiene, and delivering personalized services tailored to each client’s needs. Recognized as a VLCC professional esthiologist, Shweta ensures expert-level care and modern techniques in every service. Let us help you look and feel your best for every occasion.
