**About Shweta Makeover Studio**

We are professional freelance beauticians in Varanasi with 4+ years experience. We specialize in bridal makeup, skincare and home beauty services. Shweta Makeover Studio is dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty with elegance, precision, and care. Based in Varanasi, we specialize in professional makeup and beauty services tailored to suit every occasion—whether it’s your wedding day, a special event, or a casual celebration. Our mission is to make every client feel confident, radiant, and beautiful in their own unique way. With a deep understanding of skin tones, facial features, and the latest beauty trends, we ensure a personalized experience that highlights your best features. We use only premium-quality products and modern techniques to deliver long-lasting and flawless results. From bridal makeup to party looks and hairstyling, every service is designed with attention to detail and a passion for perfection. At Shweta Makeover Studio, hygiene, comfort, and customer satisfaction are our top priorities. We also offer convenient home services, bringing luxury beauty experiences right to your doorstep. Let us be a part of your special moments and transform them into unforgettable memories with our expert touch.
